March 22, 2021 total vaccine allocations in Florida would vaccinate slightly more than 1/4 of state population.


Moderna and Pfizer vaccines dominate current allocations and are approximately equal. The Janssen vaccine, because of its easier storage and single dose regime, as well as Merck corporation's agreement to begin manufacturing it, will likely increase in total allocation share in coming weeks.
